SERVICES WE PROVIDE

We understand that as a women you need comfort you, you need justice, you need help, you want to feel space. Justice Today is a platform that supports women who feels overwhelmed by everything that is going on. Down below is a full description of what kind of services that we give. Be blessed Queen!

Free Therapy sessions

Luckily, at Justice today free therapy is available to help individuals without resources get the support they need. Online therapy chat allows you to attend therapy sessions via chat room, text, or webcam with a licensed professional in the privacy of your own home.

Free Legal Services

At Justice today we provides free assistance to those who do not have attorneys. Services include: information and referral to legal services, individual assistance with legal document preparation; bilingual assistance and assistance in conducting self-guided legal research.

Financial Support

At Justice today provides some free financial support to women who have been affected by Injustice they faced. Services include paying Monthly Mutuelle de Sante for a period of 12 months, Support in basic needs such as food, pocket money, Water and Shelter for a period of 4 months,
